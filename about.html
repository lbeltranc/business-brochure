Our Business History

Founded in 2015 and based in Vancouver,BC. LTF Ltd. was originally established as a limited partnership by Tina Liu and Faye Liu, who discovered a great commercial opportunity: a small fast food restaurant for sale near by an ESL college in Burnaby. During the first two years, the income and costs reached its break-even point. Owners made a big change regarding to its marketing strategies: mainly target on students instead of office workers and making the price cheaper to be more competitive.

The company started making big profits since 2017 and it attracted one of our biggest investors: Laura Beltran Caicedo.She brought new receipes to the store and expanded one store to three chain.
